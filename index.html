Meet the team

Your AI staffing team. Three layers. Install who you need.

Your Orchestrator · Customizable

The Operator

Your firm's top-level orchestrator. You name it, you configure it. Applies your voice, your objection patterns, your escalation rules. Directs the Manager team and knows when to involve a human. LPU named theirs "Top Bot." You name yours whatever fits your firm.

Replaces: your ops manager tying the whole operation together.
Sourcing & Matching

Recruitment Manager

Queries your roster and public sources, ranks candidates by distance, availability, rate history, and past placements. Runs parallel outreach until a fill lands.

Replaces: 1 to 2 recruiters doing phone-tag on roster.
Compliance

Credentialing Manager

Verifies state board licenses, DEA, NPI, malpractice, and background against source-of-truth APIs. Flags expirations 90 days out. Blocks bad placements before they ship.

Replaces: a credentialing coordinator at $55 to $75k / year.
Logistics

Scheduling Manager

Owns the calendar. Books shifts, coordinates travel, handles cancellations, notifies practices when candidates are 15 minutes out.

Replaces: a dedicated scheduler at $45 to $60k / year.
Memory & Network Intelligence

Tenant Brain

Two layers working together. Your private brain stores your firm's voice, candidate history, and placement patterns, isolated completely to your instance. The network brain learns anonymized patterns across every CoverOS agency, making matching smarter for everyone, without ever exposing who you are, who your candidates are, or what rates you charge. Your data is always yours. The intelligence is shared.

Replaces: the institutional knowledge that walks out when a recruiter quits, plus the market intelligence no single agency could build alone.

FAQ

What every buyer asks on the call.

Is this real, or is this a one-person shop?

CoverOS is built and operated by Julius Black. Solo, for now. That is the point of the Founding Tenant program: the first 10 customers build the company with the founder, and you get direct access to the person writing the code. Black Label Solutions, the parent entity, converts to a Delaware LLC in Q2 2026.

What happens if I don't like it?

You walk. On day 28 of your pilot, we ask if you want to continue. If you say no, or say nothing, there is no charge and the Stripe authorization expires. The pilot is 30 days, not 30 days with a surprise on day 31. Post-activation, you can cancel anytime.

What does the AI do vs. what does my team still do?

Agents do: intake, outbound, qualification, credentialing checks, packet assembly, scheduling. Your team does: final approvals, client relationships, rate negotiation, anything requiring judgment. We do not replace recruiters. We remove the 4 hours a day they spend typing.

What about HIPAA and data privacy?

CoverOS signs a BAA before pilot data touches production. Data lives in isolated tenant databases, encrypted at rest and in transit. Your tenant brain is private and never trains any shared model. DPA available on request. SOC 2 Type I audit engaged Q3 2026.

We already use Bullhorn / GHL / Cloud Dentistry. Do we rip it out?

No. CoverOS runs alongside your existing stack and integrates bidirectionally with Bullhorn, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, and Salesforce. Your team keeps their tools. CoverOS handles the agentic work those tools don't do.

Why only 10 Founding Tenants?

10 is the number the team can personally onboard, monitor, and fix without dropping quality. At 25, we would be shipping a degraded experience. At 100, we would be shipping someone else's company. The cap is a staffing limit, not a marketing trick.

What if CoverOS breaks mid-placement?

Every agent decision is logged and reversible. Your recruiter catches anything wrong before it reaches a practice or facility. Founding Tenants have founder cell access. If a bug costs you a placement, we credit a month of Growth once paid billing begins.
